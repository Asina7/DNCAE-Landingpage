Security

All online credit cards transactions performed on www.dncae.ae using the Pay Tabs payment gateway are secured payments. The following security measures are applied to these payments:

  • Payments are fully automated with an immediate response.
  • Your complete credit card number cannot be viewed by” DNCAE “or any outside party.
  • PayTabs have built-in fraud protection system which includes in-house fraud protection layer as well as 3DSecure. This two-layered system ensures that your transactions are highly secure and hassle free.
  • All transaction data is encrypted for storage within Pay Tabs’ banks.
  • Pay Tabs is an authorized third party processor for all the major banks.
  • PayTabs at no time touches your funds; and all monies are directly transferred from your credit card to the merchant account held by DNCAE.
